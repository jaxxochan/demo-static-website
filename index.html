Manufacturing Process

Get an insight process of Saikrupa Gravure

DESIGN

All the designs at Saikrupa Gravure are extremely eye-catching and client favorite at the design department. With Design studio equipped with the latest Scanner, Imaging and White ink-based printers to get precise results on the actual substrate at Designing Level. Saikrupa Rotogravure equipped with the latest software and hardware for 3D mockup, packaging layout, color calibrated monitors and printers, Ink planning and saving the program, white ink and metallic Ink digital output.

PLATING

Copper Plating

The copper plating process uses the Copper-Plating machine also from abroad to place a layer of copper onto the cylinder for the engraving process. This compact machine, based on the latest version and technology in the rotogravure-electroplating field guarantees a high standard of workmanship.

Chrome Plating

The Chrome plating apparatus is based on the latest knowledge and technology in the rotogravure-electroplating field constructed with the latest materials gives the hardness upto 900–1000 Vickers. Controlled electrolyte flow and perfect temperature control give the cylinder a constant hardness all through.

ENGRAVING

Engraving is carried out to the phenomenal breakthrough by the latest technology in which a special diamond pen transfers the design onto the cylinder automatically using hi-speed electronic engraving ensures quality to a great extent. Or engraving machine has the touch screen LCD for Machine control, cell display and Easy Cell Calibration System.

POLISHING

The copper-finishing process uses a grinding machine for optimal quality copper surface balance in order to minimize tolerance and achieve evenness of the cylinders. The machine is a high precision machining and measuring center for rotogravure cylinders. Cylinders are simultaneously tapered and rounded with the Polish Machine gives accurate measuring for cylinder geometry and concentricity also ensures cylinder parallelism

PROOFING

To ensure our cylinders meet high-quality standards by our proofing process, All the engraved cylinders are rolled for test printing, Where Proofing Machine will take care of the colors as well as the accuracy of the design under our dedicated and passionate quality professional. We advise our customers to match their digital proofing sample with the cylinder proofing. This proofing will save our customers time and money to a great extent.

QUALITY CHECK

The quality of base cylinders plays a vital role in the successful execution of all subsequent processes of Rotogravure cylinder making, which ultimately affects the quality of print. Emerald Rotogravure maintains a very high level of Quality standards matching to international level. A fully-fledged testing laboratory with computerized color matching systems gives perfect results required by the user.

PACKAGING & FORWARDING

Proper packaging of rotogravure printing cylinders is done in lightweight fiber boxes for optimizing the transportation medium. Delivery schedules at Emerald Rotogravure are strictly adhered to so that the flawless product reaches your doorstep on time every time. Packaging ensures easy and safe transportation of cylinders at the client end. On-time delivery at the customer’s doorstep. Our infrastructure comprises transportation facilities to dispatch the packaged products. We have also tied up with many logistic & transportation companies. We do have a delivery support team that takes care of complete delivery process in coordinating with both the Customer as well as the Company.
